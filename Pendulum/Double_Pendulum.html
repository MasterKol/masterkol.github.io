This is a simulation of a double pendulum system, the differential equations that describe this system were solved using the lagrangian method. In order to expand this to longer pendulums I constructed general equations for the system and solved them here. These equations get solved in python so I currently have no way to upload it to a website, these equations work for the most part however they are unstable and the energy in the system tends towards infinity for longer pendulums.
This system is composed of 10 double pendulums offset from eachother by ±10-6 radians and quickly diverge because of the chaotic nature of the system.
