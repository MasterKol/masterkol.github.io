This program draw the Collatz fractal, from the Collatz / 3n+1 conjecture, which is an infinite fractal generated by a recurrence relation. This page was mostly made to show my friend but I figured since I was sending it I might as well make a simple page on my site.

The recurrence relation used for this fractal is

If xn is even: xn+1 = xn / 2
If xn is odd : xn+1 = 3*xn + 1

The Collatz conjecture states that any number will eventually reach one under this relation. Despite this conjecture being made in 1937 it is still unsolved and many mathematicians think its well out of our reach to make any statement about the validity of the conjecture.
I was inspired to make this by this Veritasium video on the topic.

Left drag to move around, right click to extend fractal, scroll to zoom
